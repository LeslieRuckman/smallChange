Fund the change you want to see in the world, one cent at a time.


Each time we make a purchase, we have an impact on the world around us. Although obscured by the vast complexities of our industrialized ecosystem, our purchase decisions are directly shaping the world we live in, for better and for worse. The goal of Small Change is to begin to acknowledge those complexities and find a way to create a more visible, more positive connection between the actions we take everyday and the action we want to see but may not know how to create.
